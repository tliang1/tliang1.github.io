Microsoft Store Apps


Period: January 2013 - March 2013

The Windows 8.0 (Windows 8.1 Now Available) Metro-style desktop game apps were undergraduate projects developed by a computer science student of the College of Engineering, Computer Science and Technology (ECST) in California State University of Los Angeles (Cal State LA) for the C++ Object Oriented Programming class. The developer, Tony Liang, was a computer science undergraduate.

He developed the apps using the Construct 2 game engine. He made the apps’ images using GIMP 2. They support keyboard+mouse and touch. He packaged and uploaded the apps to the Microsoft Store. He submitted the apps to the Microsoft “Ultimate Experience” promotion for a free copy of Halo 4 for the Xbox 360. He submitted the apps to the App Builder Keep the Cash Offer promotion for $100 per app.
